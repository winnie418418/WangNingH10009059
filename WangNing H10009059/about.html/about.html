關於我 About Me

自我介紹

Self Introduction

我叫汪寧，你也可以叫我Winnie。

My name is Wang Ning, you can also call me Winnie.

我的性格熱情，對世界充滿了好奇心，我最大的特點就是喜歡探索新事物，因此在不斷的嘗試下，我開發了許多愛好。

例如遊戲，攝影，繪畫，鋼琴，舞台劇，唱歌，舞蹈等等。

這麼多愛好當中我堅持最久，最喜歡，最想與別人分享的就是舞蹈。

I am enthusiastic and full of curiosity about the world. My biggest trait is that I love exploring new things. Through constant experimentation, I have developed many hobbies.

Such as games, photography, painting, piano, theater, singing, dancing and more.

Among all these hobbies, the one I have kept the longest, love the most, and want to share with others the most is dance.

我對舞蹈的想法

舞蹈對我而言不只是動作，更是情感的表達與自我的釋放。
我不是一個有著很強毅力的人，俗話說三天打魚兩天晒網。
但我對舞蹈的熱愛讓我更勇敢、更堅定，也讓我遇見更好的自己。
它正作為我的一份熱情也是一項技能，陪伴我成長。

My Thoughts on Dance

To me, dance is more than movement. It is the expression of emotion and liberation of self.
I'm not a person with great perseverance. As the saying goes, "You fish for three days and dry your nets for two days."
However, my love for dancing has made me braver and more determined, and it has also allowed me to discover a better version of myself.
It is now serving as a passion as well as a skill for me, accompanying my growth.
