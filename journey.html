成長歷程Journey

舞台劇舞蹈啟蒙Stage Play
2016-2018
兒時偶然接觸到舞台劇培訓班，在父母支持我多方嘗試、加上自身滿滿好奇心的驅使下，我展開了舞台劇之旅。學習過程中，台詞、表演、聲樂、舞蹈樣樣都不能偏廢，這的確是難得的全方位發展機會。為期兩年的珍貴回憶雖告一段落，唯有舞蹈一路延續至今。在舞台劇的舞蹈訓練裡，我真切體會到舞蹈的獨特魅力，也更加明白舞蹈對我的意義，以及我對舞蹈的重視與珍惜。
2016-2018
When I was a child, I happened to come across a stage play training class. With my parents' support for my various attempts and driven by my strong curiosity, I embarked on the journey of stage plays. During the learning process, I couldn't neglect any aspect such as lines, performance, vocal music, and dance. This was indeed a rare opportunity for all-round development. Although the precious two-year memories came to an end, only the dance has continued to this day. In the dance training of stage plays, I truly experienced the unique charm of dance, and also understood more clearly the significance of dance to me, as well as my emphasis and appreciation for dance.
拉丁舞者Latin Dancer
2016-2019
三年的拉丁舞學習，讓我的專業能力更上一層樓。我不僅完成拉丁舞六級檢定，也學習了恰恰、倫巴、桑巴、牛仔等多種舞風，並參與過無數場競賽，親身感受舞蹈賽事的獨特魅力。拉丁舞賽多以個人形式進行，必須在數十甚至上百人的場地中盡情展現自己。眾人聽著同一首音樂，卻朝著不同方向、跳出屬於自己的舞步，只靠舞姿與氣場吸引評委目光。這段經歷大幅培養了我的自信心，也在我的成長歲月中，留下濃墨重彩的一筆。
2016-2019
Three years of learning Latin dance have significantly enhanced my professional skills. Not only did I pass the Latin dance level six certification, but I also learned various dance styles such as cha-cha, rumba, samba, and jive. I participated in numerous competitions and personally experienced the unique charm of dance events. Latin dance competitions are mostly held individually, and one must fully display oneself in a venue with dozens or even hundreds of people. Everyone listens to the same music but dances in different directions, creating their own steps. They only rely on their dance posture and aura to attract the judges' attention. This experience has greatly cultivated my self-confidence and left a significant mark in my growth years.
Kpop之旅Kpop Journey
2023-至今
我對 K-Pop 的熱愛，從國中一路延續到現在。一開始只是被音樂深深吸引，後來只要旋律一響，便會忍不住跟著律動，我知道，是心底對舞蹈的熱情在驅使著我。K-Pop 舞蹈和我過去學習的風格截然不同，它兼具強烈的時代感與多元風格，也讓樂於嘗試的我不斷挑戰不同面貌。此外，K-Pop 表演極度重視團隊默契，與夥伴彼此配合、相互成就，最終才能呈現最精彩的舞台。也因此，我開始在不同場域展開表演，主要分為校園內與校園外兩大領域。
2023-Present
My love for K-Pop has been continuous since middle school. At first, I was deeply attracted by the music. Later, as soon as the melody started, I couldn't help moving along with the rhythm. I know that it is the passion for dance in my heart that drives me. The K-Pop dance is completely different from the style I used to learn. It combines a strong sense of the times with diverse styles, and it also allows me, who is eager to try, to constantly challenge different appearances. Moreover, K-Pop performances highly value team synergy. By cooperating with my partners and achieving each other's success, we can finally present the most spectacular stage. Therefore, I began to perform in various fields, mainly divided into two areas: on campus and off campus.
校內表演
School Performance
在校期間，我於各個藝術節展演舞蹈，無論國中或高中都加入了舞蹈社。國中時擔任舞蹈社副社長，高中則擔任社長。我不僅用舞蹈活躍校園氣氛，也在組織社團、策劃展演的過程中，鍛鍊了自己的領導力與組織能力。
During my school years, I performed dances at various art festivals. I joined the dance club both in junior high school and high school. In junior high school, I served as the vice president of the dance club, and in high school, I was the president. Not only did I bring vitality to the campus through my dancing, but I also developed my leadership and organizational skills by organizing clubs and planning performances.
校外表演
Outside Performance
課餘之際，我加入校外多個舞團，與一群同樣熱愛 K-Pop 舞蹈的夥伴相聚。我們不分年齡、不分性別，一同走向更大的舞台，在商場、廣場等公共場域演出，只為傳遞對舞蹈的熱愛。
In my spare time, I joined several dance crews outside of school and met a group of partners who share the same passion for K-Pop dance. We don't discriminate by age or gender and all move towards a bigger stage, performing in public places like shopping malls and squares, solely to spread our love for dance.
